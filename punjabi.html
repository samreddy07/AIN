INFORMATION ON PUNJAB STATE


Punjabi is the heart of India. Punjab is famous for its colorful life style and the world famous bangra dance. Punjab is famous for Golden temple. The language used in Punjab is Punjabis which is written using Punjabi Fonts. People of Punjab are called Sikh, Punjabi Sikh, India Punjabi, Punjabi Jatt. Their are many informative and new websites Punjab using Punjabis font. Punjabi News or news on Punjab are provided by leading Punjabi Newspapers. Punjabi Newspaper like Punjabi Ajit, Punjabi News Paper, Ajit Punjabi, Kesari, Pujab Kesari.
